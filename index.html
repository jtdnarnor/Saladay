About Saladay

At Saladay, we're passionate about bringing the rich, diverse flavors of African cuisine to a modern, health-conscious audience. Our mission is to create nutritious, delicious salads inspired by traditional recipes from across the continent.

We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, supporting small farmers and ensuring the freshest produce in every salad. Our dressings are made in-house daily, using authentic spices and recipes passed down through generations.

Whether you're looking for a quick, healthy lunch or catering for your next event, Saladay offers a taste of Africa that nourishes both body and soul.
